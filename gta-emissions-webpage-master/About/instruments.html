Urban Emissions Project Instruments

LGR Ultraportable Cavity Rindgown Spectrometer Greenhouse Gas Analyser

The LGR spectrometer is made by Los Gatos Research. It measures concentrations of methane, carbon monoxide, carbon dioxide and water vapour in the ambient air near the spectrometer. The LGR has a pump which draws air into the cavity through an external tube. Concentrations are displayed in real time by a program on the LGR itself, and can be relayed to a laptop through a serial connection.

Airmar WX 220 Weather Station

The Airmar weather station has an internal GPS which provides location and velocity parameters. It measures wind speed and direction with ultrasonic sensors and computes the wind velocity relative to the ground by performing vector math on the apparent wind and velocity of the weather station. It also outputs temperature, heading, pressure and more.

EM 27/SUN Fourier Transform Spectrometer

The EM 27/SUN Fourier Transform Infrared Spectrometer (FTIR) is made by Bruker. It makes an interferrogram of direct sunlight and a Fourier transform is then performed in order to determine the chemical composition of the atmosphere between the sun and the spectrometer.
